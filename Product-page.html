Email is the place where all business starts

Your inbox is a starting point for TeamChat communication and online meetings while also enabling you to create, share and edit documents as a team. ChatGPT integration lends a hand with smart features such as suggest a reply, make a quick overview or translate. Your notes and tasks, calendars and chats are there too. IceWarp secure cloud solution is accessible from anywhere and works in real-time, so it's suitable for remote work.

AI buddy

When you're responding to an email, ChatGPT can draft a reply based on the entire email thread. Your chatbot conversations are retained across sessions. And to quickly get the gist of a long message, ChatGPT can make a brief overview highlighting key points.

Smart multitasking

Compose multiple emails while chatting with a coworker, reviewing a document or searching through TeamChat rooms. All of this during a conference call.

Smart planning

Know immediately if you can accept a proposed meeting. Interactive invite dialog shows an overview of your schedule around the indicated time.

Smart attachments

SmartAttach uploads large files like videos or presentations to our cloud and automatically includes a link in the email. So your file is delivered fast and doesn't clog your recipient's mailbox.

TeamChat, beyond email communication

Chat in teams organized around projects, upload files, mention coworkers, comment on posted emails, pin important posts or use the embedded ChatGPT functionality. Integration with email enables sharing messages from inbox to a TeamChat room for colleagues' attention.

Faster and more straightforward than email.

Chat and invite guests to brainstorm and collaborate as if they were sitting at the same desk. IceWarp mobile helps you stay connected everywhere.

Email and TeamChat connected

Sounds basic, works like magic: share emails and incoming emails in TeamChat rooms. Compose new ones with the help of your colleagues. When you're done, you can easily view the email right from the chat room.

Seamless AI integration

IceWarp now integrates ChatGPT across its compose, chat and TeamChat message dialogs. Tap the embedded AI for help with writing a reply, for translation, to provide a brief overview of your received email, to assist with stylistic enhancement of your messages and more.

TeamChat Mobile app

All you need is a phone to follow your team's progress from anywhere. Immediately respond to new posts, comment or share files thanks to TeamChat mobile app.

Free access for all

Invite non-IceWarp free clients and contractors to TeamChat. Our guest accounts are free and unlike other services don't require registration. Simply enable free external and enable for external users as well.
